# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at **contact@bragdev.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant v2.1 is available at the link above and governs this project.
